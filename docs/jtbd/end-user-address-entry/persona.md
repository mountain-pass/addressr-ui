# End User (Address Entry)

## Who

A person filling in a web form that asks for an Australian address or a narrower location value (postcode, suburb, state). May be on a desktop or mobile device. May use a screen reader, keyboard only, or a pointing device.

## Context Constraints

- May be on a slow or unreliable connection
- May use a screen reader (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, TalkBack)
- May operate with keyboard only — no mouse or touch
- May be on a small mobile screen
- Expects results within a few hundred milliseconds of typing
- Expects to correct mistakes easily without re-typing everything

## Pain Points

- Free-text address fields produce invalid data and delivery failures
- Dropdowns that aren't keyboard accessible strand screen-reader users
- Slow or janky autocomplete feels broken
- Having to retype the whole address after a typo
